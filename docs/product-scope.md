# Product Scope

## Approved MVP

- Splash and session restoration.
- Login.
- Registration.
- Forgot password.
- Password recovery deep-link handling.
- Single-step onboarding.
- Dashboard and quick actions.
- Business settings.
- Customers list, create, edit, and delete.
- Create quotation.
- Create invoice.
- Documents list.
- Document detail and preview.
- PDF download or native viewing.
- WhatsApp sharing through Android share intents.
- AI Reply.
- AI Caption.
- Account.
- Plan and usage display.
- Loading, empty, success, and error states.

## Forbidden Scope

- WhatsApp Business API.
- Automatic WhatsApp sending.
- Chatbot or AI agent.
- Unrestricted free-prompt AI box.
- Reading WhatsApp conversations.
- Complex CRM or sales pipeline.
- Customer tags.
- Inventory beyond existing product/service list.
- Team accounts, roles, or organizations.
- Analytics dashboard.
- Billing, Stripe, or Google Play Billing.
- Push notifications.
- Complex offline sync.
- Public document links.
- PDF editor or multiple PDF templates.
- Advanced branding customization.
